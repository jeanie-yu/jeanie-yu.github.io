The Old Man and The Sea

Book Design | 2023

5.5"x 8.5"

116 pages

Laser Print on Vellum Paper

Black Ink on Printer Paper

A perfected binded book design that combines classic fiction literature with poetry using a refined version of golden ratio book layout. This brings together two masterful works: The Old Man and The Sea by the iconic Ernest Hemingway, and The Ocean by the poet Nathaniel Hawthorne. The poem was printed on vellum paper overlapping the traditional literature bringing the audience into the theme of the ocean.
