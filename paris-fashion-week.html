Paris Fashion Week

Poster Series | 2023

24"x 36"

Laser Printed on Paper

Paris Fashion Show Poster Series was designed for an annual event with international audiences. Color choice of red and blue was inspired by the French Flag. The tint and shade of the poster background choice was flowing through the time of the fashion show events with dusk and dawn. This series of posters not only promotes the show but also explores the beauty of French fashion culture.
